Who I am

Italian Front-End Developer

Studying languages has always been my priority throughout my educational path, and as a result today I can proudly claim to speak: a fluent English and Spanish, some Portuguese and a bit of French. As my language skills suggest, I had been working as a freelance translator and proofreader for 6+ years, which has always been an easy and quick way for me to get money by having fun and refining my skills at the same time. But then, at some point in 2020, something shifted in me. I felt like I needed a new educational adventure. This was when I decided it was finally time for me to learn “other ways to communicate” as well: the programming languages world started calling me!

So I started this exciting programming journey with Python at first, then I switched to the front-end side with JavaScript, HTML and CSS. I also decided to get into frameworks through React only for now, but I'm open to learn some new ones in the future. If the programming world has taught me one thing, that surely is how there’s always something new to learn in life, which I know it could be scary to some people... but I personally love it. It’s my fuel.

And last but not least, I’m a visual artist who generally loves art in all of its form. In my spare time I like making collages and illustrations from the laptop, mainly by using softwares such as GIMP and Adobe Photoshop. I've also experimented quite a lot with vector images and traditional drawings during the past couple of years.

If you want to know more about my artistic vein, just click here.
